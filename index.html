Food for Crows

An ecologically-minded crafting and farming game that explores connection with the natural and spiritual worlds.

Can you save the natural, man-made, and spirit world from ecological destruction or will the changing climate wreak havoc on you and those you care about?

The Little Ice Age was a period of time that brought major changes to the climate of North America. Civilizations that were once booming saw huge drops in population due to heavy reliance on corn, a grain that, while nutritious and easily storable, requires a predictable climate. Your goal is to rediscover the crops domesticated by your ancestors, discover new mutations, attract animals, and provide a starving population with alternatives to corn.
Along the way, you will discover that the world is not exactly what you expected. It is full of magic and spirituality, which can be harnessed for combat and exploration. While you have pressing goals to aid your people, you also must strike a balance with nature. Stripping the land of resources will eventually lead to crop failures and abandonment by animal populations.

Interact with the world

Your journey will take you to interesting locations and allow you to meet a wide variety of characters.

Manipulate the environment

The world is every changing and you can impress your will upon it! Use tools to change the soil type and build irrigation.

Engage in combat

This game is not all sunshine and rainbows! You will need to get your hands dirty. Fight a variety of enemies and defend your friends.

Grow crops

There are many crops that grow and interact with each other. You will need to learn what crops need and how to benefit from their products if you want to survive.
